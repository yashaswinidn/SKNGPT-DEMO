About Shri Kengappa Nayaka Government Polytechnic

Our Journey

Established in 2022, Shri Kengappa Nayaka Government Polytechnic has become a cornerstone of technical education in the region. Our institution is dedicated to providing high-quality education, fostering innovation, and preparing students to excel in their careers.

Over the years, we have expanded our course offerings to include a wide range of diploma programs, each tailored to meet the evolving demands of the industry. Our committed faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and strong industry connections ensure that our students receive the best possible education and hands-on experience.

Our Mission

"Our mission is to provide quality technical education and holistic development opportunities to rural polytechnic students. Through innovative teaching methodologies, industry-aligned curriculum, and hands-on training, we aim to equip our students with practical skills and ethical values. We are committed to nurturing a learning environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and social responsibility, preparing our graduates to excel in diverse fields and contribute meaningfully to society."

Our Vision

"To empower rural polytechnic college students with knowledge, skills, and opportunities that faster innovation, entrepreneurship, and sustainable development in their communities and beyond."
